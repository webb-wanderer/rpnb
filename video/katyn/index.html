Im Wald von Katyn (1943)

(In the Forest of Katyn)

Documentary of the 1940 Katyn massacre, at which 4,400 captured Polish officers were murdered by the Soviet political police of the NKVD. Shows the crime scene in 1943 and the exhumation of bodies and personal belongings found on the bodies of Polish officers from a mass grave in the Katyn forest.

Duration 18:43.
